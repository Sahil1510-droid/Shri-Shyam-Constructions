Maintenance Guide
Fabric Care Tips
🧺
Washing
Delicate fabrics like silk and velvet require dry cleaning. Linen and cotton can be machine-washed on gentle cycle at 30°C. Always check label.
☀️
Sun Exposure
Rotate curtains seasonally to prevent uneven fading. UV-resistant linings protect fabric in south-facing rooms. Silk is most vulnerable.
♨️
Ironing
Steam-iron linen and cotton while damp. Velvet should only be steamed — never pressed flat. Use a pressing cloth for silk and organza.
📏
Measurement
For fullness, use 2–2.5× the window width. Floor-length curtains should break 1–2 cm on the floor for a luxurious pool effect.
